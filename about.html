What is AHAND WITH A DIFFERENCE (AWAD)

AHAND WITH A DIFFERENCE (AWAD) is a non-government organization with registered office situated at Post office Building Kampala, in the Republic of Uganda. The NGO was registered by a group of social workers in accordance with Uganda’s company act in 2012.

Our vision

A society where all vulnerable people are spiritually vibrant, enjoy good health and are productive

Our Mission

To nurture the growth and development of vulnerable groups in society by improving access to healthcare and education, guaranteeing a better livelihood for all.

Our Core values

We are genuinely concerned with the vulnerable people in society and we focus on the people receiving appropriate high quality care services. We have five core values:

1.Respect for human Dignity; we respect the sanctity and dignity of all we serve.

2.Excellence; we pursue excellence in all our activities and dealings.

3.Equity; we strive for equitable care and observe fair dealings in all our activities at all times irrespective of race, color, ethnicity and creed.

4.Integrity; we commit ourselves to the highest and professional standards in all we do.

5.Accountability; we strive to meet all the transparency indicators and be accountable for every resource of our beneficiaries.

The objectives of the organization are to;

· Increase access and utilization to education and health care services.

· Provide appropriate solutions to livelihood challenges

What We Do

Education and Capacity building

Assuring the universal right to education for every Ugandan is the constitutional responsibility of the government. The technical program emphasizes education to all vulnerable groups of people through partnerships, policy influence and advocacy to support the government to deliver on its mandate in the education sector. The technical program invests in targeting the vulnerable groups in a population such as prostitutes, disabled, the disadvantaged and others. It ensures that they can at a minimum have access to formal education and training in vocational skills. These skills include tailoring, catering crafts making, cosmetology, fashion and design etc. Providing these groups capacity is intended to put them at the same plane with the other society people to ably compete for any arising opportunities and better their society. The community focused technical model is further enhanced by the support offered by individual gifts in kind, company social cooperate responsibility projects, and funders.

Basic Healthcare

We emphasize access to health care by all the vulnerable groups we work with, by investing in and carrying out projects to enable and enhance access and utilization of medical care by vulnerable groups. Our community focused model, with support from funders, strengthens access to basic healthcare through medical camps, provision of medicine and strengthening government endorsed awareness campaigns.

Livelihood

As responsible citizens blessed with the position and opportunity to create positive social benefits, we are committed to sharing God’s love with those in need in our communities termed as livelihood. As a result, the team is contributing towards shelter; basic scholastic materials to those in need. We are more concerned with the most vulnerable in society. As a result we have set up a fund and we look forward to growing the fund targeting more unprivileged Ugandans.
